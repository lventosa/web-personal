Hello, I'm Laura!

I graduated in Economics at Universitat Pompeu Fabra and I'm currently studying Mathematics at Universitat de Barcelona.

My current interests include, but are not limited to, (computational) statistics in any of its forms and, in particular, the transition to the Julia programming language for data science.
